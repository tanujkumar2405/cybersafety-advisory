About CyberSafety Advisory

A student-led initiative committed to ethical, transparent cyber safety education and advisory support

CyberSafety Advisory is a student-led cyber fraud awareness and advisory initiative. We focus on ethics, transparency, and responsible digital safety education.

We are a group of dedicated individuals passionate about helping people stay safe online. Our team brings perspectives from cybersecurity education, student communities, and lived experience of supporting fraud victims.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

We provide calm, practical advisory guidance to people affected by cyber fraud. Our goal is to help individuals understand incidents, assess risk, and take safe next steps ‚Äî all while respecting privacy and maintaining ethical boundaries.

Our Vision

A world where people feel empowered to navigate cyber fraud safely, understand their risks, and protect themselves with confidence. We believe education and transparency are foundational to digital safety.

Who We Are

Our Team

CyberSafety Advisory is powered by cybersecurity students and volunteers certified in CSCU & ECSS (EC-Council). We are not affiliated with any government agency, bank, or law enforcement organization.

Key Characteristics:

  • Student-led: We are powered by students and young professionals committed to education
  • Non-profit in spirit: We operate freely, asking for no money or personal data
  • Independent: We are not affiliated with banks, government, or law enforcement
  • Ethical: We prioritize privacy, transparency, and honest communication
  • Practical: We focus on actionable, real-world guidance
  • Certified: Our team holds relevant cybersecurity certifications

Our Values in Practice

Privacy Is Sacred

We collect no data, use no trackers, and do not share information with third parties. Your information stays private and confidential. We do not retain data permanently.

You Stay In Control

You remain in control of all decisions and accounts. We advise; you decide. You file reports yourself. You contact authorities directly.

Transparency Matters

We are honest about what we can and cannot do. We never overstate our capabilities or make false promises. We operate within clear ethical boundaries.

Education Builds Safety

We believe understanding fraud is your best defense. We focus on education, awareness, and practical prevention strategies.

What We Will NOT Do

To maintain our ethical boundaries, we will NEVER:

  • Ask for OTPs, passwords, PINs, or CVV codes
  • Access or attempt to access your accounts
  • Handle money or process payments
  • Guarantee recovery or refunds
  • Impersonate banks, police, or government agencies
  • Use high-pressure sales tactics
  • Collect or retain personal data
  • Track your online behavior
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Misrepresent our affiliations or credentials
