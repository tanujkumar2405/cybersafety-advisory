Common Questions About Contact

We collect no data, use no trackers, and do not store information permanently. We are a static website with no backend database. All form interactions happen client-side ‚Äî your information never reaches our servers. You can inspect our code (it's all HTML, CSS, and JavaScript) to verify this.

The form is for your convenience if you want to reach out. However, since we don't store data, submitting the form doesn't actually send us an email or store your information. We recommend using email directly: cybersafetyadvisory+contact@gmail.com

We are a student-led volunteer initiative. We do our best to respond, but response times depend on team availability. We prioritize urgent situations and work as quickly as possible. For active fraud situations, please contact your bank and law enforcement immediately ‚Äî they can help faster than we can.

Once we receive your message, we will review it and respond with guidance tailored to your situation. Our response will include practical next steps, official resources, and clarification on what we can and cannot help with. We will never ask you to share sensitive information or money.

We can help by providing clarity, guidance, and resources. We can explain what happened, suggest safe next steps, and direct you to official channels. However, we cannot access accounts, recover money, or guarantee outcomes. Official authorities (banks, police) handle actual recovery. We maximize your chances by helping you take the right actions.
