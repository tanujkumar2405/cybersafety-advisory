About CyberSafety Advisory

A student-led initiative committed to ethical, transparent cyber safety education

Our Mission

We provide calm, practical advisory guidance to people affected by cyber fraud. Our goal is to help individuals understand incidents, assess risk, and take safe next steps ‚Äî all while respecting privacy and maintaining ethical boundaries.

Our Vision

A world where people feel empowered to navigate cyber fraud safely, understand their risks, and protect themselves with confidence. We believe education and transparency are foundational to digital safety.

Who We Are

CyberSafety Advisory is a student-led cyber safety and advisory initiative. We are a group of dedicated individuals passionate about helping people stay safe online. Our team brings perspectives from cybersecurity education, student communities, and lived experience of supporting fraud victims.

Key Characteristics:

  • Student-led: We are powered by students and young professionals committed to education
  • Non-profit in spirit: We operate freely, asking for no money or personal data
  • Independent: We are not affiliated with banks, government, or law enforcement
  • Ethical: We prioritize privacy, transparency, and honest communication
  • Practical: We focus on actionable, real-world guidance

Why Student-Led?

Our student-led approach brings unique advantages:

  • Relatable perspective: We understand generational challenges and communication styles
  • Accessibility: We speak a language our peers understand, without institutional jargon
  • Passion, not profit: We are motivated by helping people, not financial gain
  • Continuous learning: As students, we stay updated with evolving threats and solutions
  • Community-driven: We build trust through transparency and genuine care
